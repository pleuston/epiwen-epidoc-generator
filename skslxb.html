石刻史料新編 contents

石刻史料新編 (Shike shiliao xinbian) — the 新文豐出版公司 (Xinwenfeng) reprint compendium of premodern Chinese epigraphy collections, issued in four series (輯). Its contents below are drawn from Kuhn & Stahl 1991, an annotated bibliography of the set; each work links to its page in the premodern sources register.
