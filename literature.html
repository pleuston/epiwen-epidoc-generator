The 金石學 literature — premodern Chinese epigraphy catalogues (著錄) and gazetteers that record inscriptions. Each work opens its 序/跋/四庫提要 and its inscription list (目錄); titles cross-link to the inscription concordance. Harvested from Kanripo (四庫全書).
