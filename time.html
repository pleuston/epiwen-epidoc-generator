Select a dynasty or reign era.

Dynasty and reign-era dates follow the tables in Zhongguo lishi niandai jianbian 中國歷史年代簡編 (Academia Sinica standard) and Endymion Wilkinson, Chinese History: A New Manual (Harvard-Yenching Institute). BCE dates are stored as negative integers and converted to ISO 8601 proleptic Gregorian for EpiDoc (1 BCE = year 0000).

Time authorities with persistent URIs:
DILA Time Authority — East Asian dynasties and reign eras with calendar conversion, maintained by Dharma Drum Institute; the primary authority for this corpus.
PeriodO — international linked-data gazetteer of scholarly period definitions; Chinese coverage is growing and its URIs are used in cross-domain archaeological datasets.
iDAI.chronontology — German Archaeological Institute (DAI) period service, CIDOC-CRM modelled, interoperable with PeriodO and Getty AAT; useful for mapping Chinese periods onto global archaeological timelines.
Wikidata — dynasty and era Q items carry P580/P582 start–end claims and link to VIAF, GND, and Library of Congress, providing a lightweight cross-reference anchor.
