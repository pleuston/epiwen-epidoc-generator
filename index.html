Epiwen EpiDoc for Chinese stone inscriptions / 中文碑刻 EpiDoc 生成器

A bilingual, client-side tool for producing valid EpiDoc / TEI records for Buddhist stone-sutra inscriptions of North China. Developed for the Altergraphy / Epiwen encoding project. Nothing is uploaded — the XML is built live in your browser.
